The Forecast Foundation

The Not-for-Profit Foundation Behind Augur

The Forecast Foundation is a group of developers and other technology professionals who are passionate about the potential of decentralized applications. It does not own or lead Augur, but rather supports and develops the free, open-source protocol that is Augur.
